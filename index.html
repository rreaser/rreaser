Robert Reaser

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Economics at the University of California, Davis. My research is in energy and environmental economics with an emphasis on the industrial organization of wholesale electricity markets.

My current research combines high-frequency electricity market data with microeconomic theory, econometrics, and engineering models to examine how data centers and grid-scale storage are reshaping electricity markets.

You can contact me at rreaser@ucdavis.edu.
